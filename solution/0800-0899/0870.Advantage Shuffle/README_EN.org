Given two arrays A and B of equal size, the advantage of A with respect
to B is the number of indices i for which A[i] > B[i].

#+begin_html
  </p>
#+end_html

#+begin_html
  <p>
#+end_html

Return any permutation of A that maximizes its advantage with respect to
B.

  </p>
#+end_html

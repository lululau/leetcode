#+begin_html
  <pre>

  <code>Sales</code> table:

  +---------+------------+------+----------+-------+

  | sale_id | product_id | year | quantity | price |

  +---------+------------+------+----------+-------+ 

  | 1       | 100        | 2008 | 10       | 5000  |

  | 2       | 100        | 2009 | 12       | 5000  |

  | 7       | 200        | 2011 | 15       | 9000  |

  +---------+------------+------+----------+-------+



  Product table:

  +------------+--------------+

  | product_id | product_name |

  +------------+--------------+

  | 100        | Nokia        |

  | 200        | Apple        |

  | 300        | Samsung      |

  +------------+--------------+



  Result table:

  +------------+------------+----------+-------+

  | product_id | first_year | quantity | price |

  +------------+------------+----------+-------+ 

  | 100        | 2008       | 10       | 5000  |

  | 200        | 2011       | 15       | 9000  |

  +------------+------------+----------+-------+
